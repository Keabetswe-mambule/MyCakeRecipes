About App

Giving you power to bake better



Virtually everybody knows how to bake something. Otherwise we’d all be starving to death. It’s an important life skill. Many of us can only make the basics like scones or cookies. However, there are a bunch of baking recipes out there that can improve our capabilities in the kitchen. With MyCakeRecipes one can have all thier favourites recipes in one place

Bake more with ease



With the mojo of this being "Do the baking while the app does the managing" it only clear how the app intends to bring conviniece and organized baking lifestyles in its users lifes. MyCakeRecipes is one of the most popular and consistent baking apps. It features a lot of the basics features. You can sift through tons of recipes, save your favorites, and check out a lot other stuff. It’ll give you a prep time, a main ingredient, and the meal you’re wanting to make.
